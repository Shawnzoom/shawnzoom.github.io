101 Data Science projects you can do on your laptop

Inspired by the Coursera Data Science Specialization I completed, I started this repo to create and/or collect 101 data science projects that can be done on a laptop.That is, all computation takes place on the laptop.

Why am I doing this?

  1. I wanted to make sure I continued to develop my data science skills
  2. To inspire and help others who are interested in data science
  3. To demonstrate it is possible to do useful data science without big data

Why 101 projects?

  1. It is a play on the college course numbering system, as in level-100. I want the projects to be accessible to a wide audience
  2. To encourage myself to get the community involved. 101 is a lot of projects for one person

Guiding principles

The following outlines the guiding principles for the individual projects.

  • Made available under an MIT license
  • Project should be reproducible with freely available programming languages, tools and data
  • Must include R markdown file, Jupyter or other freely available notebook
  • Project format should roughly follow the basic science fair lab report:
    • Title
    • Your name
    • Purpose
    • Question(s) or Hypothesis
    • Methodology/Experiment
    • Results
    • Conclusion
    • References
